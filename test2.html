About Our Institute

Coding Research Centre (CRC), Hazaribag is a premier computer science institute committed to shaping the future of aspiring coders and IT professionals. Situated in the heart of Hazaribag, CRC provides a well-rounded learning environment that bridges academic theory with practical application. The institute offers specialized training for students of Class XI and XII (both CBSE and JAC boards) as well as for undergraduate and postgraduate students pursuing BCA and MCA.

At CRC, students are trained in a wide range of high-demand technologies and programming languages including C, C++, Java, C#, Python, and Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA). The curriculum also extends to web development technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, along with Android app development and Database Management (Oracle). Our goal is to ensure students not only understand the concepts but also gain the skills to apply them confidently in real-world scenarios.
